Big Little Planet

This project (which name is definitely not a reference to any PlayStation game) is about planet generation. The existence of the World, has always amazed me. Including all its concepts: the views, the sky, the lands, the sea, etc. Trying to replicate all these ideas was my motivation to enter the world of Computer Graphics. Actually, my proposal for my final's degree project was to create an "Ecosystem Generator", where you could create your own planet with your own properties such as vegetation or weather. Obviously it was too ambicious for a final's degree project, so I ended up droping the idea... until now.
